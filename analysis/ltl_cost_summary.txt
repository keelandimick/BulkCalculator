IMPLICATIONS FOR B2B PRICING
----------------------------
The $361.24 average freight cost creates a significant fixed cost that must be 
distributed across the order quantity. This makes MOQ critical for maintaining margins.

- 25 units: $14.45/unit (59% of total cost at $10 product cost)

RECOMMENDATIONS
---------------
1. Set minimum MOQ at 100 units to keep freight manageable
2. Implement tiered pricing to incentivize larger orders
3. Consider freight-included pricing for simplicity
4. Offer free freight threshold at $2,000-2,500
5. Provide custom quotes for very large orders (1000+ units)
